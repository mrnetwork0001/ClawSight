#  ClawSight (Trade Audit Terminal)
### *Audit Your PnL. Roast Your Bias. Built for the Binance Builder Competition 2024.*

**ClawSight** is the first agentic AI dashboard designed to tell you the mathematical truth about your trades. Using the **OpenClaw SDK**, it retrieves actual Binance market data and audits your trade performance—grading you on your timing, risk management, and market awareness.

##  The Mission: "Stop Providing Exit Liquidity"
Most traders think they bought a "dip" or sold a "top," but mathematical reality often tells a different story. ClawSight takes your **Entry** and **Exit** prices and compares them to the true local high/low of that 20-minute window.

If you bought the absolute ceiling, our AI gracefully calls you a **"FOMO Casualty."** If you snipe the bottom and sell the peak, it crowns you a **"Sniper."**

##  Core Features
- **Dual-Moment Audit**: Provide both **Entry** and **Exit** timestamps (optional) for a full historical deep-dive into your trade timing.
- **Relaxed "Truth Filter"**: We accommodate memory blur. Input prices up to **50% off** market reality, and our AI will still provide a professional, precise audit (flagging as 'Estimated' only after that).
- **Shareable PnL Cards**: Generate a high-quality, Binance-branded performance card in one click. Perfect for sharing your "Sniper" or "Safe Profit" logic on Social Media.
- **Global Risk Monitor**: A real-time market scanner that detects liquidity sweeps, volatility spikes, and bearish signals across the top Binance pairs.
- **Top/Bottom Accuracy**: Mathematically proves how close your entry/exit were to the local market extremes.
- **Binance Pro Aesthetics**: A "Glassmorphism" UI with 3D Rendered Card Flips, native theme-consistent calendar pickers, and zero-latency interactions.

##  Tech Stack & Specialized Skills
- **Frontend**: React + Vite (Ultra-Stabilized)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4 + Framer Motion (3D Rendered Card Flips)
- **Agentic Core**: OpenClaw SDK (Binance Market Data Pipeline)
- **Backend**: Vercel Serverless (Custom-optimized for zero-latency Binance Vision API calls)
- **Image Generation**: html-to-image + downloadjs for client-side Proof-of-Performance cards.

##  How to Audit Your History
1. **Initiate Audit**: Input your Trading Pair (e.g., BTCUSDT).
2. **Context Input**: Provide your Entry Price and Exit Price.
3. **Audit History**: Use the native **Calendar Pickers** for your Entry and Exit moments (optional).
4. **The Verdict**: Receive your performance grade (Absolute Sniper, Safe Profit, Liquidity Donor, etc.) directly from the OpenClaw AI engine.
5. **Export & Share**: Click **"Export PnL Card"** to save your verified performance snapshot.
