# 🦞 ClawSight (Trade Audit Terminal)
### *Audit Your PnL. Roast Your Bias. Built for the Binance Builder Competition 2024.*

**ClawSight** is the first agentic AI dashboard designed to tell you the mathematical truth about your trades. Using the **OpenClaw SDK**, it retrieves actual Binance market data and audits your trade performance—grading you on your timing, risk management, and market awareness.

![ClawSight Trade Audit](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/antigravity-ai/assets/main/clawsight-audit-banner.png) *(Placeholder: High-end Trade Audit Terminal Screenshot)*

## 🚀 The Mission: "Stop Providing Exit Liquidity"
Most traders think they bought a "dip" or sold a "top," but mathematical reality often tells a different story. ClawSight takes your **Entry** and **Exit** prices and compares them to the true local high/low of that 20-minute window.

If you bought the absolute ceiling, our AI gracefully calls you a **"FOMO Casualty."** If you snipe the bottom and sell the peak, it crowns you a **"Sniper."**

## ✨ Core Features
- **⚡ AI Performance Roast**: Get immediate, human-readable feedback on your trade logic. Was it a smart accumulation or just panic?
- **🎯 Top/Bottom Accuracy**: Mathematically proves how close your entry/exit were to the local market extremes.
- **📉 Realized PnL Analysis**: Dynamic, color-coded performance tracking for your historical or real-time trades.
- **🛡️ Multi-Metric Context**: All AI verdicts are backed by RSI, Volatility, and Price Action snapshots—providing a learning loop for every trade.
- **💎 Binance Pro Aesthetics**: A "Glassmorphism" UI optimized for ultra-performance and high-stakes trading.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Specialized Skills
- **Frontend**: React + Vite (Stable)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4 + Framer Motion (3D Rendered Card Flips)
- **Agentic Core**: OpenClaw SDK (Binance Market Data Pipeline)
- **Backend Infrastructure**: Vercel Serverless (fra1 Region for zero-latency API access)
- **Live Intelligence** via Binance Data-Vision (vision endpoints)

## ⚙️ How to Audit Your History
1. **Initiate Audit**: Input your Trading Pair (e.g., BTCUSDT).
2. **Context Input**: Provide your Entry Price and Exit Price.
3. **Optional History**: Provide a timestamp (YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM) to audit an old trade, or leave it blank to audit a live session.
4. **The Verdict**: Receive your performance grade and improvements directly from the OpenClaw AI engine.
